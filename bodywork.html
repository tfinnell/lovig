Comprehensive Bodywork

Even though Heather describes the types of energy work she does as separate: Angel Care Bodywork, Reiki and Energetic Clearing, this is done solely for the benefit of the reader to illicit a better understanding of what comprises her healing work.

It is Heather's experience that even though there are different types of energy work, all are intrinsically related and just another facet or expression of the same healing energy. Therefore when she works with someone, her work can extend beyond just Reiki, Angel Care and Energy Clearings. It depends on the client and the work that needs to be done. Heather can tailor her healing work to fit your needs.

Types of Bodywork

Angel Care Bodywork

Angel Care Bodywork is a term that Heather has developed to describe the roles, ongoing assistance and energy that the angels contribute to the healing process.

When Heather is working with a client, many times their angels are present, guiding, channeling through her and working alongside her to bring healing and balance. The angels are very powerful, special beings and have their own significant touch and powerful energy that they bring to the session.

Reiki Massage

Reiki is an energetic healing modality in which Heather channels life force energy through the laying of the hands to restore physical, emotional, mental and spiritual balance to a person. It was developed by Mikao Usui in the 1900's in Japan and is an extremely powerful healing method.

Energetic Clearing

Energetic Clearing is energy work that directly focuses on clearing, cleansing and removing residual energy from a person's auric field.

Often times people can pick up energy from places they've been, absorb mental and emotional energy from other people and have their own residual energy stuck in their field that is ready to leave.

In a session, Heather will clear these energies as well as any other energies that don't belong in the person's aura. She will then give specific personalized information and tips on things her client can do to maintain their cleared energy field.

Disclaimer

Heather is not a licensed health care practitioner nor does she have any medical training. Her work is purely focused on the energetic components of the body.

Although some have experienced the added bonus of an alleviation of certain physical symptoms, this type of bodywork is not meant to replace professional medical treatment if that is what's needed.

Energetic bodywork has been known to manifest emotional and physical symptoms in certain individuals and is something that can't be predicted but depends on the individual person and what was worked with energetically in the session.

Most times this is apart of the healing process. However if there are any concerns that come up, Heather provides a free 20 minute follow up session, in which she can provide further counsel and guidance on how to process, work with, and integrate what has surfaced from the session.
