Why get a reading?

Often times the underlying reason why many people come for a reading is because they are looking for hope that things will be o.k., wanting to feel security in their life or are looking for answers that will alleviate their fears.

Heather knows that to open yourself up and ask questions about your life can emote feelings of vulnerability. Therefore when she gives a reading, a safe container is created and everything spoken between you and her stays confidential.

Types of Readings

Heather's reading style is eclectic and mainly composed of these three components when she gives a reading:

Psychic Abilities

Heather receives psychic information through her clairvoyance (images, symbols, colors), clairaudience (hearing voices and words), clairsentient (feeling) and claircognizance (knowing) abilities. She is also an Empath (can feel/sense the emotions of a person, place or any living thing) and can work with your past lives.

Mediumship

Heather often times will receive and channel messages from many different sources such as: the Divine, spirit guides, deities, ascended masters, the deceased, angels and power animals, to name a few. She acts as the translator, relaying the messages of guidance to the person receiving the reading.

Spiritual Guidance/Counseling

As a part of the reading process, not only does Heather channel messages and use her psychic gifts to answer your questions, but also helps you get to the root of why you are asking a question, provides counsel and guidance with the answers once received and addresses additional resources for how to integrate the information into your life. It is Heather's experience that to have a successful reading, this is a necessary and important part of the process.

Expectations

Heather Is Not A Fortune Teller.

Although she does have the gift of foresight, she will not give readings that are solely composed of prediction questions or future based questions.

The intention behind her work is to help others with healing, guidance, and clarification through her gifts as a psychic, channel, medium, and spiritual counselor. It is when someone comes to the session really looking for guidance and help in their life that the readings are the most accurate and effective.

Sometimes a connection can't be made.

Many times a person comes to a reading with the expectation that all of their questions will be answered or that they'll be able to communicate with their deceased loved one, spirit guides, etc.

It is important for you to know that sometimes a question asked, can't be answered. This could be because a clear reading can't be made of the energy around the person, situation, or your not meant to know the answer.

Also, if you are looking to connect with a specific type of energy such as a certain spirit guide or deceased loved one, Heather will make "the call" for them to be present, however it is ultimately up to that energy to respond back and communicate.

How To Prepare For A Reading

Drug Policy

Please abstain from using drugs or alcohol 48 hours before the reading. Drugs and alcohol can cloud a person's energetic field, which can make it a hard to give an accurate reading and for the person to absorb the information. If you are taking a necessary prescription drug that doesn't impair your functioning or mental processes, this should be fine.

Questions

Have specific questions in mind, really focusing on the most pertinent ones. Also, don't ask a question you really don't want the answer too, this is important and something you must differentiate on your own.

Invitations

If you would like to connect with a deceased loved one (person or pet), your spirit guide, angels, etc. as apart of the session, Heather suggest to invite them to be apart of the reading before attending the session. This isn't necessary but does make it more likely that they will show up because the person themselves has requested it.

Caveats

Future Based Questions

Heather can see the future, however what she sees is based on the way you are projecting your energy in that current moment in time as well as how others are projecting their energy, in relation to the question. Therefore what is seen during the reading can potentially change and a different outcome can happen based on the circumstances of life. Also, based on the question, she may see one or many probable futures, and if this is the case, can give you guidance on how to make the most ideal possibility a reality for yourself.

Questions About Others

Heather has the upmost respect for another person's privacy on a psychic level. Therefore when a question is asked about another person, she will first check in with their spirit and get permission before continuing. Sometimes access isn't granted or only limited information is given or can be read.
