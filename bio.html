Heather Lovig holds a B.A. in psychology and an M.A. in women's spirituality. She is a lifelong psychic and medium who works with the elemental, angelic and "other spirit" realms.

Her background is in reiki, multidimensional bodywork and spiritual guidance. She has been on the shamanic path since she was a little girl. Through lovingly assisting others on their spiritual path, Heather offers support and gentle guidance for those who are ready. She has been reading professionally for over 10 years and relies solely on her gifts and divine guidance from Spirit.
